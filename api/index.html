Popular Endpoints

Get All Companies

GET /api/?endpoint=companies&api_key=YOUR_API_KEY

Retrieve a list of all companies in the database with their complete details.

Search Companies

GET /api/?endpoint=search&term=SEARCH_TERM&api_key=YOUR_API_KEY

Search for companies by name, industry, or location.

Get Company Details

GET /api/?endpoint=company&id=COMPANY_ID&api_key=YOUR_API_KEY

Get detailed information about a specific company by its ID.
